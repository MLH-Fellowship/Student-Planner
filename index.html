What is StudyScape?

It is a platform for students to manage their class notes along with their class schedule integrated with the google calendar and track their tasks using to-do lists.

1 - Store your class notes

Store your class notes and access them whenvever you want according to the class schedule and tasks.

2 - Integrated with Google Calendar

Your class schedule and events are displayed using the google calendar so that you don't miss out on them.

3 - Track your tasks using to-do lists

Manage and track your tasks using interactive and easy to manage to-do lists.
